Foux Foundation

Foux Foundation is a private charitable foundation that was set up in 2015 by members of the Foux family.

Welcome

Welcome to Foux Foundation

​
Throughout their lives, Cyril and Leila were always incredibly generous to friends, family and strangers alike.

The Foundation is their legacy.


​Foux Foundation was named after Cyril and Leila Foux – it was founded with their capital and run by their grandchildren.

What we fund

Criteria

  1. Income of less than £3m per annum
  2. Must be a registered charity
  3. No length of track record is required (although viability of your organisation and team to deliver the proposal will be assessed)
  4. Any geographical location

Areas of focus

  1. The relief of sickness and promotion of good health
  2. The advancement of education
  3. The advancement of disadvantaged young people
  4. The prevention or relief of poverty
Our Work

Our Work

The foundation is focused on helping in the areas of: health, education, youth opportunity and poverty.


Living across three different continents, the trustees take a global approach and are keen to support the appropriate organisations regardless of location.

HOW TO GET IN TOUCH If you have a question about our work or whether your organisation is suitable for Foux Foundation funding, please email at info@fouxfoundation.org

If you think your proposal is suitable, please submit it using the submission form below. The style and format of your proposal is up to you but please try to include the following information:

  1. Organisation history and activity
  2. Team structure
  3. Progress and impact to date (quantitative where possible)
  4. Full proposal or brief (this can be funding for a project or operational costs)
  5. Budget breakdown
  6. Funding to date

Physical proposals will not be reviewed

Due to the volume of proposals received, we are unfortunately unable to respond to those that are not considered.
